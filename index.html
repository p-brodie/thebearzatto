Using "The Bear" for my
Final Foodways Research Project

I am an English Education major attending Western Illinois University (WIU). For my final project, I researched FX's "The Bear," and created several blogs and a cornerstone project explaining my findings, then coded this website. The show is brilliant, accuracte, and has accumulated a fanbase beyond foodies. I would highly recommend this show for any restaurant or drama show fans. Follow the Blogs dropdown menu to explore my findings and thanks for stopping by.
